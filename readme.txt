Understanding sales and profits generated from your Easy Digital Downloads Store with ease!


== Description ==

**The Zendesk Chat Widget via API Plugin offers additional reporting functionalities for your Easy Digital Downloads store.**
 
* Sales and profit data by country, product, vendor, customer
* Rich search filters to zoom in on the data as needed 
* Possibility to save custom reports

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hwGYkWXaorY]

**Like this plugin?** Consider leaving a [5 star review](https://wordpress.org/support/view/plugin-reviews/edd-enhanced-sales-reports?filter=5).

**Plugin compliant with EDD 3.0**

= Features =

* Sales / Profits per Product
* Sales / Profits by Country
* Sales / Profits by Customer
* Sales / Profits by Vendor	
* Filters by Country, Product, Vendor
* Filters by Country, Product, Vendor
* Filters by Sales, Commissions and Profits	
* Creation of Saved Reports (Up to 2)

1. Activate the plugin

2. Go to Downloads > Reports and click on Enhanced Sales Reports Tab
